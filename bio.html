Take me back

Christian Hall is a producer and the founder of Two Independent Eyes, a development and production company whose clients have included Warner Bros., Disney/ABC, and Amazon. He develops and produces work across film, television, and live immersive theater. He is a graduate of the University of Southern California.

Hall is executive producing the upcoming features TRUST THE MAN, UNSPEAKABLE, and NDA. His producing credits include POPULAR THEORY, MERRY GOOD ENOUGH, and COSMIC FLING, among others.

Through Two Independent Eyes, he is developing a slate of film and television projects that includes the folk-horror feature THE DAMNING OF A COUNTRY MERCHANT; the crime-comedy BIG GIRL FACE; the mystery-thriller AND THEN SHE WAS GONE, based on Karen McQuestion's novel The Moonlight Child; and the unscripted adventure competition series VANISHED.

He is also creating The Hotel Pharmacy, a multi-day immersive experience built around personal exploration and the possibility that checking into a hotel might change your life.
